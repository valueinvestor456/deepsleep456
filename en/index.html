More Recommendations

Nearby Cafes Worth Visiting

Deepsleep456 is not far from well-known cafes like WTF Coffee Camp, Jungle De Cafe, and Ai Nara Cafe — convenient for guests who want to stop for coffee with a view during their trip.

WTF Coffee Camp

A popular camping-style cafe right by a waterfall — enjoy your coffee amid the sound of the falls and fresh air, with plenty of great photo spots and a golf-cart shuttle service.

Jungle De Cafe

A well-known cafe with hammocks right by the waterfall, in a shaded, relaxed setting — perfect for listening to the water and resting your eyes on green views.

Ai Nara Cafe

A beautifully designed Japanese-style cafe set in a valley on the way to Mon Jam, with stunning mountain and meadow views and a cool atmosphere all day long.
