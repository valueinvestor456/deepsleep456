Why "Deep Sleep" at Deepsleep456?

Deepsleep456 sits at higher elevation in Pong Yaeng, near Mon Jam, which naturally keeps it cooler than Chiang Mai city all year round. Daytime stays pleasantly cool and breezy — you'll barely need the air conditioning. At night the temperature drops noticeably, and in some seasons it's cool enough to want a warm bowl of moo kratha (Thai BBQ) with the family. That cool, quiet mountain air is exactly what helps you sleep deeply — the experience the name Deep Sleep is all about, and something you won't find in the middle of the city.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Might Want to Know Before Booking

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is after 2:00 PM and check-out is before 11:00 AM. If you need an earlier or later time, just ask us via LINE in advance.

How many guests can stay?

It's a private whole-house rental with 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, sleeping up to 12 guests — great for families and groups of friends.

How much does it cost?

From 1,000 THB / person / night — the actual rate depends on group size and season. Message us on LINE to check prices and availability.

Is there a swimming pool?

No swimming pool — but there are mountain-view bathtubs both indoors and outdoors to soak in instead.

Can we have a BBQ / Thai hotpot (moo kratha)?

Yes! There's a dedicated picnic sala for BBQ and moo kratha with your group.

Is parking available?

Yes, free private parking within the property.

Are pets allowed?

Please ask us via LINE before booking — our team will let you know the conditions.

How do we get there? Is it far from the city?

About 38.6 km from Chiang Mai Airport. Easy to reach by car, or use our airport transfer service (extra charge). The house is close to Mon Cham and several famous cafes.

Any other questions? Ask us on LINE — we reply fast.
